About Andrea

Hi, my name is Andrea Gonzales!
Born and raised in New York City, I'm passionate about programming, video games, music, comic books, and social activism. Currently, I'm a computer science and journalism double major at UNC-Chapel Hill. I'm the co-creator of the viral video game Tampon Run, and more recently, the co-author of the book Girl Code: Gaming, Going Viral, and Getting it Done. It's being published by HarperCollins Publishers and comes out March 7, 2017. Check it out, and scroll down to learn more about me!
